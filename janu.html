RVR Sarovar Portic Dindi

Dindi - By the Godavari is one of the premium and luxurious riverside resorts in Dindi. Ideally located on the banks of the River Godavari, the Resort offers spectacular views and a lavish manicured garden. The rooms are spacious and elegantly designed with modern furnishings and amenities. The Resort is ideal for corporate events as well as weddings and other get-togethers. Indulge in relaxing massages and other therapies at their lavish spa. It is a great weekend getaway destination for fun and relaxation.
Location: Sivakodu Ramarajulanka Rd, Ramarajulanka, Andhra Pradesh 533253


FOOD

Flavors Restaurant at the RVR Sarovar Portico serves a range of local and multicuisine treats. Our hotel is not just limited to local cuisines and serves an array of multicuisine delicacies too. The Konaseema Cuisine which consists of local delicacies like Prawn Biryani, Country Chicken with Egg Curry is a must-try when you visit Dindi. Experience a fine dining while tasting the treats of the land at our restaurant in Dindi.


ROOMS

  • Take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool or stroll around the lush green lawn.
